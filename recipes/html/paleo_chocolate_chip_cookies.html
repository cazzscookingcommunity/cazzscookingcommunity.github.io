Paleo Chocolate Chip Cookies


Category: Sweets

Serves: None

Prep Time:

Cook Time:

Ingredients:

  • 1 egg (room temperature)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup coconut oil
  • 1/2 cup coconut sugar
  • 1 cup almond or hazelnut flour
  • 1/4 cup coconut flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3/4 cup choc chips (diary free if required)
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180C.
  2. Line a baking sheet and set aside.
  3. Melt coconut oil and set aside (should not be hot when adding other ingredients.
  4. ) In a bowl mix flours, salt, sugar and baking soda.
  5. Whisk to mix and remove any lumps.
  6. In the cooled coconut oil add the vanilla extract and egg.
  7. Whisk until combined.
  8. Pour wet ingredients into dry and mix until dough forms.
  9. Fold in 1/2 cup of choc chips.
  10. Place Table spoon amounts on baking tray and sprinkle a couple of extra choc chips on top of each cookie.
  11. Cook for 8-10 minutes or until golden.
  12. Cool on tray then remove to a cooling rack.
  13. Store in an airtight container once completely cold.
  14. Yum yum!.
