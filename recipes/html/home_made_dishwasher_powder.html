Home made dishwasher powder


Category: Household

Serves: None

Prep Time:

Cook Time:

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups washing soda
  • 1 cup citric acid
  • 1 cup salt

Instructions:

  1. Mix together and store in airtight container.
  2. Use 1-2 TBSP per load.
  3. You can made your own washing soda by spreading out desired quantity of Baking Soda and cook in oven for 1 hour.
  4. Stir once half way.
  5. You can tell if its ready because it will change colour slightly.
  6. You can buy washing soda in the clothes washing isle of the supermarket, but it comes in a plastic bag.
