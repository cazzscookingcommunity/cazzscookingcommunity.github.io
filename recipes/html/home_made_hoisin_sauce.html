Home Made Hoisin Sauce


Category:

Serves: None

Prep Time:

Cook Time:

Ingredients:

  • 8 TBSP Gluten Free Soy Sauce
  • 5 TBSP peanut butter
  • 3 TBSP honey
  • 1/4 cup fresh green onions chopped
  • 1/2 TBSP chilli flakes
  • 4 teasp rice wine vinegar
  • 1 TBSP garlic powder
  • 3 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 1 TBSP chilli paste

Instructions:

  1. Place all ingredients in a small sauce pan and cook for about five minutes on low or until all ingredients combined and thickened slightly.
  2. Store in airtight container.
