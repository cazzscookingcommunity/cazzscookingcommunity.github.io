Vegetable Bake


Category: Sides

Serves: None

Prep Time:

Cook Time:

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 Sweet potatoes chopped
  • 400gms pumpkin chopped
  • 400gms butternut pumpkin chopped
  • 1 red and yellow capsicums chopped
  • 1 red onion cut in 1/8
  • 1/2 cup feta crumbled
  • 200 gms sundries tomatoes chopped
  • 1/2 bunch coriander leaves removed
  • Olive oil
  • salt and pepper (cracked pepper)

Instructions:

  1. Place all ingredients except coriander and salt and pepper in oven dish and mix to coat all.
  2. Seal with foil and cook in preheat moderate oven for 30-40 minutes.
  3. Remove foil and stir well.
  4. Return to oven for a further 15minutes.
  5. Remove from oven and season to taste, then stir well.
  6. Sprinkle with coriander and serve with meat dish if desired.
