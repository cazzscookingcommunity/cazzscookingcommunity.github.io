Easy Hiking Breakfast


Category: Breakfast

Serves: 1

Prep Time: 5 mins

Cook Time: 2 mins

Ingredients:

  • 1 small ziplock bag
  • 2 Wheatbix (GF if required)
  • 2 TBsp powered full cream milk
  • 2 teasp sugar
  • 1/4 cup water ( added at camp site)

Instructions:

  1. Add to the ziplock bag wheatbix, powdered milk and sugar.
  2. Bring one for each morning for your hike.
  3. Add 1/4 cup water directly to the bag and massage to mix.
  4. Eat directly out of the bag.
