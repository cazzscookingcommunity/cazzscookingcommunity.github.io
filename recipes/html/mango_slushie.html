Mango Slushie


Category: Drink

Serves: None

Prep Time:

Cook Time:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen mango pieces
  • 1 cup cold jasmine tea
  • Mint leaves
  • Frozen lime slices

Instructions:

  1. Wiz tea and mango in blender until done.
  2. Pour into Long glass or two smaller glasses, and garnish with lime and mint.
  3. Serve immediately.
  4. You can sweeten with honey if desired.
