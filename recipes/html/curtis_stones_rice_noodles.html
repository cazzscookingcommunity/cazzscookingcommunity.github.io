Curtis Stones Rice Noodles


Serves:4

Diet:GFO, DiaryFree, EggFree, LowSugar

Category:Poultry, Dinner, Lunch

Prep Time:15 mins

Cook Time:15 mins

Ingredients:

  • 500gms mince chicken
  • 200gms Pad Thai rice noodles
  • 2-3 carrots grated
  • 4 spring onions chopped
  • 2-4 garlic cloves crushed
  • 1/4 cup Oyster sauce
  • 2 TBsp Soy sauce (GF if necessary)
  • 1 TBsp chilli sauce
  • 1 teaspoon cornflour, mixed with 2 TBsp water
  • Olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook Pad Thai noodles per packet and instructions and sprinkle olive oil over them and mix (this stops the noodles sticking together) then set aside.
  2. Meanwhile mix all sauces together, with cornflour mixture and set aside.
  3. Heat wok or large pan and add olive oil.
  4. Heat oil for a few minutes before adding garlic.
  5. Cook garlic for 5 minutes then add mince chicken.
  6. Cook chicken until fully cooked then add carrot, sauce mixture and noodles.
  7. Stir to combine then add salt and pepper and top with spring onion.
  8. Serve immediately with additional spring onion on top of each bowl.
