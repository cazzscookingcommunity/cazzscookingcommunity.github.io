Smart Cookies


Serves:24

Category:Snack, Dessert, Breakfast

Prep Time:15 mins

Cook Time:30 mins

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cereal
  • 2 cups SR flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon bicarbonate soda
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 150 gms butter melted
  • Zest one orange
  • 1/2 cup orange juice (1 orange)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180C.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl and stir through orange zest.
  3. Pour in butter and orange juice, mix until just combined.
  4. Make TBSP balls from mixture and place evenly on lined cookie tray.
  5. Press balls down slightly with the back of a fork.
  6. Place in oven for 20-30 mins or until golden brown.
  7. Once completely cool store in airtight container up to a week.
  8. Freeze extras for later.
