Moroccan Fries


Category: Sides

Serves: None

Prep Time:

Cook Time:

Ingredients:

  • 10 white potatoes
  • Moroccan spice
  • Olive oil spray

Instructions:

  1. Heat oven to 180C spray oven tray with olive oil spray.
  2. Cut spuds into 1/8 and spread out evenly on tray.
  3. Spray them with olive oil spray and sprinkle generously with Moroccan spice.
  4. Place in oven for 15 minutes remove and flip them and give the tray a little shake before returning it to the oven for another 15 minutes or until golden.
  5. Serve hot with sweet chilli sauce if desired.
