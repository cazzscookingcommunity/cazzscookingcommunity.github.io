Vegan Chocolate Cake


Serves:8

Category:Dessert

Prep Time:15 mins

Cook Time:40 mins

Ingredients:

Cake:
  • 1 1/2 cups GlutenFree flour*
  • (*Replace with plain flour if desired)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup pure cocoa
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 TBspoon vinegar
Icing:
  • 1 1/2 cup icing sugar sifted
  • 2 TBspoons cocoa sifted
  • 1 TBspoon Nutalex/Margarine melted
  • 1-2 TBspoons Boiling water
  • cheat by using Betty Crockers prepared icing ** not appropriate for vegan or diary intolerant.

Instructions:

Cake:
  • Grease cake pan and put grease proof paper in the bottom and set aside.
  • Turn on oven to 180C.
  • Sift dry ingredients and stir to combine.
  • Pour in wet ingredients and stir to combine.
  • Pour into prepared pan and cook for 30-40 minutes or until skewer comes out clean.
  • Cool for 10 minutes and turn out into a plate.
  • Let cool completely before icing.
    • Icing:
  • Stir dry ingredients together and add Nutalex.
  • Add water one TBspoon at a time until you get your desired consistency.
  • Spread over cooled cake and decorated with what you desired ( MandMs, shredded coconut, fresh berries, sprinkles are a few examples.)
