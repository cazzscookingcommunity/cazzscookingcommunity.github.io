Black Bean Salad


Category:

Serves: None

Prep Time:

Cook Time:

Ingredients:

Main:
  • 1 can black beans rinsed and drained
  • 1 can corn drained
  • 1 red capsicum diced
  • 3-4 spring onions chopped
  • 1/2 bunch parsley finely chopped
Dressing:
  • 3 TBsp olive oil
  • 1 TBsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Main:
  1. Put all Salad ingredients in a medium bowl and stir well to mix.
  2. Place dressing ingredients in a small jar and shake well.
  3. Pour dressing over salad just before serving and mix well.
Dressing:
  1. mix in jar and shake.
