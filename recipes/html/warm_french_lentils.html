Warm French Lentils


Category: Lunch, Dinner

Serves: None

Prep Time:

Cook Time:

Ingredients:

Main:
  • 1 cup green or French lentils
  • 3 cups water
  • 4 bacon rashes chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic chopped
  • 2 rosemary sprigs leaves removed and chopped
  • 2 TBsps capers chopped
  • 1/2 cup parsley chopped
  • 2 chicken stock cubes
  • 2 TBsp olive oil
  • salt and pepper taste
Dressing:
  • 3 TBsp olive oil
  • 1 TBsp grainy mustard
  • 1/2 TBsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teasp dried sage

Instructions:

Main:
  1. Soak lentils in water with a little salt for 6-8 hours.
  2. Once soaked crush with your fingers the stock cubes in lentils and stir well.
  3. Place on heat and cook for 20-25 minutes.
  4. Add extra water if it becomes dry.
  5. Stir occasionally.
  6. Once done drain and return to pot.
  7. Meanwhile heat olive oil in a fry pan and cook bacon, until crispy and brown.
  8. Add garlic and cook for a further 3-5 minutes.
  9. Add rosemary and cook for a further minute or two.
  10. Turn off heat and add dressing and capers.
  11. Stir well then pot into lentils.
  12. Add salt and pepper to taste and parsley and stir well.
  13. Serve with crusty bread or as a side.
Dressing:
  1. Mix in jar and pour over.
