Gratin


Category: Dinner

Serves: None

Prep Time:

Cook Time:

Ingredients:

  • 500gms minced lamb
  • 1 TBsp olive oil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 red capsicum chopped
  • 1 carrot grated
  • 1 zucchini grated
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1/4 cup shredded basil
  • 400ml Passata
  • 1 cup grated cheese
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1 cup rice that has been rinsed and soaked for 20 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180C.
  2. Heat oil in large pan and cook garlic for 4 minutes.
  3. Add meat to pan and cook until no longer pink.
  4. Add capsicum and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Add rice and cook for a few minutes.
  6. Add carrot, zucchini, passata, paprika, water and basil.
  7. Give it a good stir and place in a baking dish.
  8. Sprinkle cheese so it covers the entire top.
  9. Place in oven for 30 minutes.
  10. Serve with crusty bread and/or salad, if desired.
