Larb


Category: Dinner

Serves: None

Prep Time:

Cook Time:

Ingredients:

  • 850gms minced pork
  • 3 TBspoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves crushed
  • 1 inch ginger grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon chilli powder
  • 1-2 limes juiced
  • 1/3 cup fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 cup mint leaves chopped
  • 3 cups rice noodles cooked and chopped
  • 1 lettuce leaves removed
  • Extra limes to serve

Instructions:

  1. Prepare lettuce leaves and set aside.
  2. Combine lime juice, fish sauce and sugar, stir to combine and set aside.
  3. Heat oil in pan and cook meat until brown.
  4. Add garlic, ginger and chilli and cook a few minutes.
  5. Pour in juice mixture and stir through with noodles.
  6. Sever inside a lettuce cup with lime on the side.
  7. Alternately serve in told with thinly sliced cumber, lettuce and some chilli sauce.
