Three Ingredient Cookies


Category: Sweets, Snack

Serves: 24

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 8 mins

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup peanut butter (smooth)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180C.
  2. In a mixer combine all ingredients and mix until combined.
  3. Scoop out 1 TBSP of mixture and roll into a ball.
  4. Place on a line baking sheet.
  5. Once sheet is full use the back of a fork to flatten each cookie.
  6. Bake in oven for 8-10 minutes.
