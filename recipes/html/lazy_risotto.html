Lazy Risotto


Category: Poultry, Dinner

Serves: 4

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 35 mins

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3-4 rashes bacon finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves crushed
  • 500gms chicken thighs chopped into cubes
  • 1 teaspoon grated lime rind
  • 1/2 teaspoon chilli powder
  • 140 gms tomatoe paste
  • 1 1/2 cups arborio rice
  • 750ml chicken stock
  • 1/4 cut chopped parsley
  • 2-3 spring onions chopped

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in oven proof dish and cook bacon until nicely brown.
  2. Add garlic and cook for a few minutes until golden.
  3. Cook chicken in batches until all done.
  4. Add rice to pan and cook a few minutes, stirring continuously until all coated.
  5. Add all other ingredients except spring onions and stir to combine.
  6. Put lid on and place in preheated moderate oven for 35 minutes.
  7. Remove from oven and sprinkle with spring onions.
  8. Serve with salad if desired.
  9. * alternately you can replace the lime and parsley with lemon and Rosemary for a different taste.
