Okonomiyaki


Category: Lunch

Serves: None

Prep Time:

Cook Time:

Ingredients:

Main:
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 160 gms chicken stock
  • 2 spring onions chopped
  • 1/8 cabbage shredded
  • 1 zucchini grated
  • 1 small carrot grated
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying
Optional:
  • Bacon
  • Pickled ginger
  • Shrimp
Toppings:
  • Spring onions chopped
  • Kewpie mayonnaise
  • Okonomi sauce (or teriyaki sauce)
  • Fried shallots

Instructions:

Main:
  1. Whisk eggs in a large bowl, add flour, stock and salt and pepper and whisk to combine.
  2. Stir in all other ingredients.
  3. Heat oil in large pan and place about 1/2 cup of mixture making it as round as possible.
  4. You can place an amount of bacon and/or shrimp on pancake before flipping.
  5. Once edges start to cook flip over and cook other side.
  6. Flip over a couple of time to ensure middle cooked and both sides are golden.
  7. Remove from pan and garnish with sauces and spring onions and/or bacon if desired.
  8. Serve hot.
Optional:
  1. Add these as sides or you can mix in with the eggs.
Toppings:
  1. Squeeze sauces all over top and sprinkle Shallots and Spring Onions on top.
