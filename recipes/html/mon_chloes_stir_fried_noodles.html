Vegetarian Stir Fried Noodles


Serves:4

Diet:GlutenFree, EggFree, DiaryFree

Category:Dinner, Lunch

Prep Time:15 mins

Cook Time:15 mins

Ingredients:

  • Flat rice noodles
  • Stir Fries Veggies
  • Bean shoots
  • Coriander 1/4 cup chopped
  • 4 onions chopped
  • 2 cloves crushed
  • Oil
  • Sauce
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 TBsp each sweet chilli
  • 1 TBsp oyster sauce
  • 1 TBsp fish sauce
  • 1/2 lime juice

Instructions:

  1. Make sauce and set aside.
  2. Cook noodles per packet direction, then set aside.
  3. Heat wok and heat a small amount of oil.
  4. Cook the garlic for 3 minutes.
  5. Place veggies in pan and stir fry until veggies cooked.
  6. Place noodles in the pan with the sauce.
  7. Stir together until blended and heated through.
  8. Serve with bean shoots, spring onion and coriander sprinkled on top.
