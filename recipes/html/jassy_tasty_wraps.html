Jassy Tasty Wraps


Serves:None

Diet:

Category:Lunch

Prep Time:

Cook Time:

Ingredients:

  • 1 sheet puff pastry
  • Tasty cheese cut in 1 inch x 2 inch strips
  • Ham
  • Dijon mustard

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180C.
  2. Cut puff pastry sheet into 6th on each piece spread mustard, then place a small amount of ham and top with cheese.
  3. Being two corners together and squeeze so the corners stay together.
  4. Brush with egg or milk.
  5. Place on a tray lined with paper.
  6. Pop in oven for 15 mins or until golden brown.
  7. Serve hot- yum!.
