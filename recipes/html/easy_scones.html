Easy Scones


Serves:20

Category:Dessert, Snack

Prep Time:30 mins

Cook Time:1 hrs

Ingredients:

Plain scones:
  • 4 cups SR flour
  • 300 mls cream
  • 300 mls water
  • Pinch salt
Date scones:
  • 250 gms dates chopped
  • 3 TBsp sugar

Instructions:

Plain scones:
  • Turn on oven to medium (180D).
  • Grease a large flat tray and set aside.
  • In a large bowl stir the dry ingredients.
  • Pour in the cream and water and stir with a KNIFE until combined.
  • Do not over stir! Tip out to a generously floured surface and turn once to flour all sides.
  • Gently push down to desired thickness about thumb joint high.
  • Cut out with scone cutter or small glass and place close together on tray.
  • ( Unlike cookies you want them touching.
  • ) Cook until just starting to change colour, about 20-40 minutes.
  • Turn the tray around a few time to ensure all sides brown evenly while cooking.
  • Sever with whipped cream and jam and hot cup of tea!.
    • Date scones:
  • As above but adjust as follows: Add sugar to the flour and mix.
  • After water and cream is added to the flour add the chopped dates.
  • Stir with a knife until combined.
