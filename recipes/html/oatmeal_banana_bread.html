Oatmeal Banana Bread


Category: Sweets

Serves: None

Prep Time:

Cook Time:

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs beaten
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 2 cups mash bananas
  • 1 1/2 cup oat flour*
  • 1 cup oats
  • 8 dates chopped and blended
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder, baking soda and cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Heat oven to 170C.
  2. Grease and line a baking dish, set aside.
  3. Mix wet with ingredients and dry ingredients in seperate bowl.
  4. Add wet ingredients to dry and stir well.
  5. Pour milk bro prepared tray and cook for 30-40 minutes or until golden brown.
  6. * make oat flour by placing oats in a blender and blend on high for 1-2 minutes.
