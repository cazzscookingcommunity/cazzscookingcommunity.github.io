Grandmas Bread and Butter Pud


Category: Sweets

Serves: None

Prep Time:

Cook Time:

Ingredients:

  • 6 fruit scones cut in half*
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 TBSP sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • Handful of sultanas (optional)
  • Butter and Jam

Instructions:

  1. Grease a pan with butter or margarine and set aside.
  2. Heat oven to 175C.
  3. Butter both cut sides of scones and one side with jam.
  4. Put together like a sandwich and cut into quarters and place in pan until all scones used up.
  5. Sprinkle over sultanas.
  6. In a bowl beat eggs with milk, sugar and essence.
  7. Pour milk mixture over bread and place in oven for 20-30 minutes.
  8. There should be no wet bits when done.
  9. Cover with foil if getting too brown while cooking.
  10. Serve alone or with cream and/or ice cream.
  11. if using bread (not fruit scones) butter and jam each slice and place one piece on top of the other, like you are making a sandwich.
  12. Cut each sandwich into 16ths, and place in pan.
  13. If using plain bread I would definitely throw in a handful of sultanas!.
