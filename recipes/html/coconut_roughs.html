Coconut Roughs


Category: Sweets

Serves: None

Prep Time:

Cook Time:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup desiccated coconut
  • 3/4 cup sifter icing sugar
  • 1/2 cocoa (good quality)
  • 1/3 cup dried milk powder
  • 125gm melted coconut oil
  • Extra coconut

Instructions:

  1. Melt coconut oil in a small saucepan then remove from heat.
  2. Add all other ingredients and mix well.
  3. Dollop teaspoon amounts on a lined tray.
  4. Sprinkle a small amount of desiccated coconut on top.
  5. Place in freezer for 10 minutes.
  6. Remove from pan to a air tight container.
