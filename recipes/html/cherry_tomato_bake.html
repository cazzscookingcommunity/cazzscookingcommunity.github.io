Cherry Tomato Bake


Serves:

Category:

Prep Time:

Cook Time:

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups cherry tomatos halved
  • 3 cloves garlic minced
  • 2 TBsp olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon Salt and Pepper
  • 1/2 cup feta crumbled
  • 1 bunch dill chopped

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180C while preparing the ingredients.
  2. In shallow baking dish place all ingredients.
  3. Cook for 30-40 mins.
  4. When tomatoes have all collapsed remove from oven.
  5. Stir and sprinkle with additional dill if desired.
  6. Serve with BBQ meat.
  7. * different variety tomatos can be used, I prefer 2 red and 1 yellow punnet.
