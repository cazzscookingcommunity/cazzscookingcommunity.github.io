Asian Salad


Category:

Serves:

Prep Time:

Cook Time:

Ingredients:

Main:
  • 2-3 Bok Choy bunches washed and chopped
  • 100 gms cashews
  • 1/2 avocado chopped and mixed with 1/2 lemon juice
  • 1 punnet tiny tomatoes chopped in half
  • 1 Lebanese cucumber peeled and sliced
Dressing:
  • 2 TBsp white wine vinegar
  • 2 TBsp castor sugar
  • 1 TBsp soy sauce
  • 1 teasp seasame oil
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

Instructions:

Main:
  1. None
Dressing:
  1. In a small sauce pan heat vinegar and sugar until sugar dissolves, about 5 minutes.
  2. Remove from heat, then add all other dressing ingredients and pour into a jar.
  3. Close jar with lid and give it a good shake and place in fridge until ready (this can be made a day early-If you do this remove from fridge before cutting up salad and shake well before adding to salad.
  4. ) Add salad ingredients to a bowl or platters and drizzle well with dressing.
  5. Serve with easy chicken dinner from previous post.
